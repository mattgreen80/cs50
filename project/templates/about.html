What is CreditCar?

CreditCar is a stock and finance management system for dealers and a website where customers can browse a dealer's vehicles and apply for finance.

We make it easy for anyone to browse a dealer's vehicle stock and apply for finance on any car they choose! Dealers can also see and edit their stock and view finance applications on their vehicles.

CreditCar is based in Auckland, New Zealand.
